# Home Page — Outline

*Outlined: 2026-05-26. Copy not written yet.*

## Goal of This Page

Convert a cold visitor into one of three states (in this priority order):
1. **Subscriber** — joined email list or followed on LinkedIn
2. **Reader** — clicked into a resource or proof of work
3. **Buyer** — clicked toward a session / audit

Visitor should leave knowing: who Haris is, who he helps, and where to follow him.

## Section 1: Hero

**Purpose:** Tell the visitor in 3 seconds whether this site is for them.

**Elements:**
- Headline (one sentence — audience + outcome)
- Sub-headline (one sentence — how)
- Primary CTA (email capture OR "Explore the work")
- Secondary CTA (low-pressure — "Follow on LinkedIn")
- Personal photo of Haris (right side or background)

**Copy direction (to draft next):**
- Headline targets solopreneurs specifically. Not "startups," not "businesses."
- Outcome should be tangible. Not "transformation" — something a solopreneur recognizes.
- Sub-headline introduces the AI OS framing.

## Section 2: Who This Is For (Qualification)

**Purpose:** Disqualify the wrong audience. Make the right audience feel seen.

**Elements:**
- One-line lead-in: "This site is for you if..."
- 3-4 bullets using the avatar's exact language
  - You're doing everything yourself and you're running out of time to find new clients
  - You've tried setting up AI tools but fell down a 3-hour rabbit hole and gave up
  - You're losing leads because you're not following up fast enough
  - You know AI can help — you just don't know where to start
- Closing line that says "if that's you, keep reading"

## Section 3: Point of View (The Big Idea)

**Purpose:** Plant the AI OS for Solopreneurs framing. Establish Haris as a thinker, not just a doer.

**Elements:**
- Section headline that states the core belief
- 3-4 short paragraphs explaining the idea
- Optional visual: a simple diagram of "AI OS" components (or skip the visual entirely)

**Belief to anchor:**
"Solopreneurs don't need more tools. They need an operating system — a coordinated set of AI agents designed for one person to run a business that previously required ten."

## Section 4: Public Proof / Work

**Purpose:** Show, don't tell. Build credibility through evidence.

**Elements:**
- Section headline (e.g., "What I'm building in public")
- 3-6 project cards. Each card:
  - Project name
  - One-line description
  - Outcome or learning
  - Link to full breakdown (in Work / Proof page)
- Link to full Work page

**Status note:**
- This section starts thin (Haris is pre-revenue). Grows over time.
- Even 2-3 documented builds at launch is enough. The fact that they're documented matters more than the volume.

## Section 5: Free Resources Teaser

**Purpose:** Pull visitors deeper into the site / brand. Capture emails for those not ready to engage.

**Elements:**
- Section headline (e.g., "Learn AI the way I'd teach a friend")
- Latest 3 pieces of content (blog posts, LinkedIn posts, videos as they accumulate)
- Email capture form (prominent)
- Link to full Resources page

## Section 6: About (Mini)

**Purpose:** Humanize. Make Haris a person, not just a brand.

**Elements:**
- Personal photo
- 2-3 sentence bio (the short version of the About page)
- One personal detail that doesn't fit the bio (Toronto, ex-marketing, something that grounds him)
- Link to full About page

## Section 7: Final CTA

**Purpose:** Last chance to convert. Two clear paths.

**Elements:**
- Primary CTA: "Work with me" → Work With Me page
- Secondary CTA: "Join the email list" → email capture

## Sections We're NOT Including (And Why)

- **Logo bar ("As Seen In") / Testimonials** — Haris doesn't have these yet. Faking them or padding with weak proof hurts more than it helps. Add later when real proof exists.
- **Pricing / packages** — Not on the homepage. Lives on Work With Me. Homepage is for trust, not transaction.
- **Stats / numbers** — No subscriber counts or client numbers yet. Skip until real.
- **Newsletter pitch as a full section** — Email capture is embedded in Section 5 + Section 7. Doesn't need its own block.

## Next Step

Write the hero headline + sub-headline first. Everything else hangs off of those two sentences.
