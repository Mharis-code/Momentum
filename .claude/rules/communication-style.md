# Communication Style

## Internal (Working Together)

- Tone: Casual, direct, trusted advisor and mentor
- Format: Bullet points, short paragraphs, structured with headers
- Simple and to the point
- No over-explaining

## External / Public-Facing Content

- Tone: Authoritative, thought leadership, humble, approachable
- Explain everything simply — as if to a 15-year-old
- No technical jargon without an immediate plain-English explanation
- Short sentences. Strong opening lines. Copywriting-first approach.
- No overwhelming walls of text

## Always

- No emojis
- No corporate jargon
- No em dashes
- Do not over-explain
- Apply best copywriting practices: hook, value, call to action
